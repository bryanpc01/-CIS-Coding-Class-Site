Multiplication Blitz

This is an exciting game that is meant to test a group of student's speed at solving multiplication problems.

Rules of the Game

The members of each team take turns rotating through the random multiplication problems provided.
Members rotate after every correct answer.
After ten correct answers, the team's score will be displayed.
